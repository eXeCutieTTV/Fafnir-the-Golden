suffixes beginning with vowels: suffix vowel overwrites the verb stem vowel, if applicable. If the verb stem vowel was emphatic, the suffix vowel transforms to its emphatic equivalent, if applicable.

suffixes beginning with consonants in parentheses: consonant only appears on verb stems that end in vowels.

suffixes beginning with vowels in parentheses: vowel only appears on verb stems that end in consonants, unless that consonant is a post-vocalic resonant, in which case the vowel is optional.
